Core Technology Areas

Explore our key technology domains that power our innovative solutions.

Step 1: Park and Power

Effortlessly park your EV directly over our discreet wireless charging pad, seamlessly integrated beneath the parking spot. No need for manual connection—simply position your vehicle, and charging begins automatically.

Step 2: Magnetic Magic

Our revolutionary double D coil configuration, integrated into the Ground Assembly (GA), creates a robust magnetic field. This innovative design guarantees smooth and efficient power flow, optimizing the charging process for your EV's battery.

Step 3: Resonant Connection

Inside the Vehicle Assembly (VA), the receiver coil syncs with the magnetic field, creating a strong bond called magnetic resonant coupling. This bond allows power to flow smoothly without any physical contact, charging your EV effortlessly and reliably.

Step 4: Charging Made Easy

When the magnetic field resonates, it generates electricity in the receiver coil. Our wireless system is designed to adapt, accommodating up to 25% misalignment. This means you can enjoy convenient charging, even when parking isn't perfectly aligned.
